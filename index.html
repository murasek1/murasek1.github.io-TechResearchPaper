Adobe Systems

If you are interested in creative activities, you have probably heard Adobe.

Adobe Systems Incorporated founded in 1982, is the leading producer of desktop publishing and graphics software used by creative professionals. Not only for professionals but also for in-house and freelance designers, Adobe provides software such as Adobe InDesign and Adobe Photoshop. They also offer a plethora of enterprise-level publishing products as well as Adobe type products.

Adobe Software

Meanwhile Adobe has developed Adobe Acrobat, Adobe illustrator, and PDF as core technology, they also invented high quality technology such as InDesing, Photoshop, and Flash. Those technology, like PDF and Adobe Flash Player, have ubiquitous and functionality. Due to their ability to meet market's request, they became world wide platform. In addition, they possess Macromedia;Web contents and broadcasting technology, and Omniture;Web analysis and optimizing tools.

Adobe Creative Cloud

Adobe offers their products through cloud technology (Hosted on Amazon Web Services). Adobe Creative Cloud is a service offering from Adobe Systems, Adobe's cloud, that gibes users access to collection of software developed by adobe. Those contents include graphic design, video editing , web development , photography, and cloud services. User of Adobe Creative Cloud can download software from the Cloud through the Internet. The software is installed directly on a local PC and used as long as the subscription remains valid.

Adobe Flash Player

Adobe Flash Player is software used to stream and view video, audio and multimedia and Rich Internet Applications on a computer or supported mobile device. Flash Player runs Small Web Format files, and Adobe Flash file format for displaying "animated" vector graphics on the web. SWF file formats allow audio, video, and several other possible interaction with the end user.
